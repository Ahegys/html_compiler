A Brief History of AI
The Beginning
The field of artificial intelligence research was founded on the claim that human intelligence "can be so precisely described that a machine can be made to simulate it."
The Development
AI research has explored a number of approaches to creating intelligent systems, including symbolic reasoning, neural networks, and evolutionary computation.
The Future
The future of AI is uncertain and full of potential. As technology continues to advance, the possibilities for AI are endless.
